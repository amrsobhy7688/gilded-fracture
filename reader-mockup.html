Prologue · Episode 0 The Ledger

In the year 643 post-Unmaking, your name is not written in ink.

Your name is written in light.

To be a citizen of the Cyriath Empire in good standing is to exist inside a ledger so vast and so precise...

...that the Ministry of Sanctity knows, within a margin of three percent, exactly how much aether your household consumed before you finished your morning tea.

The contribution of your life, your work, and your house — noble or common — to the Empire's illuminated whole is your reputation, your credit, and your proof that you belong to the light.

Cyritium is the capital of that ledger.

Where Lunaris mines...

...Solaris trades...

...and Cyritium accounts.

The great houses of the city do not make things. They verify them. They authorize them.

This crystal is sanctified.

This shipment is legitimate.

This person is in good standing.

This light is holy.

The Celestial Ledger, maintained in the Office of Holy Order beneath the High Spire...

...is the longest unbroken document in the known world...

...643 years of every crystal that has ever moved through Imperial hands...

...recorded in the precise, unhurried handwriting of a hundred generations of House Mccord auditors.

The Empire does not run on faith alone.
It runs on faith and receipts.
